About Me

My name is Sara Taylor. Never in a million years did I think I would be in a Full-Stack web design certification program. But here I am making the leap!

I had been working on my own website partime in Wordpress for several months when my 19 yo came home from his own business. I was hoping for some help on how to make my site ADA compliant from his web-developer. My son finally said "Mom, your questions to 'So-n-so' made him have to go in and fix our website. You should take a class and learn how to do this from scratch."

He reminded me that I was good at math, languages and had the mind of a designer. So here I am ...lets get this party started.
